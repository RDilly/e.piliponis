About

Elise Piliponis is an actress and model from Nashville, TN who now lives and works in Boston, MA. She is currently majoring in Theatre and Journalism at Northeastern University. She has been involved in theatrical productions for over ten years, gaining extensive experience both on and off stage. She has modeled for several photographers in and around Nashville.
